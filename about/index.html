About Luling Huang

Luling's research examines different organizing principles in processes of change in various contexts. His dissertation compares and tests four mathematical discrepancy models of belief change using experimental methods. Another aspect of his research explores the interrelationship between the content and process of online political discussion. He obtained his Ph.D. in Media and Communication at Temple University.
